Brownie Recipe

  1. prep work

    • preheat the oven
    • rest the butter to room temperature
    • wash your hands

  2. mix dry ingredients

    1. In a large mixing bowl, add two and a half cups of all-purpose flour, one cup of sugar, and one cup of cocoa powder. Stir ingredients thoroughly.
    2. Add in one tbsp of baking powder. Also one tsp of baking soda
    3. Set aside.

  3. mix wet ingredients

    1. In a medium bowl, break two large eggs, whisk until consistent.
    2. Add one tbsp of vanilla extract and half a tsp salt to the egg mixture.
    3. Pour a half cup of milk into the wet mix

  4. combine mixtures

    1. Place the large mixing bowl with dry ingredients in a stand mixer on low setting.
    2. Slowly pour the wet ingredients.
    3. Drop 8 tbsp of room temperature butter into the mixer.

  5. Pour and bake

    1. Grease an 8 by 8 in pan
    2. Pour mix into the pan.
    3. Place in oven and bake for 20 minutes or until toothpick is clean

  6. Enjoy
